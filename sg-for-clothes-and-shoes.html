SECURITY GUIDE FOR CLOTHES AND SHOES

The working shoes are hugely important since in autumn, winter and spring, there is a high chance for the slippery. Warm and good quality shoes in this case are needed to keep the workers protected from sliding. The working clothes has to be warm enough and not really heavy that the workers do not get sick due to the cold weather. A high-quality clothes material is in need. The workers have to get cotton clothing under the working clothes. The working clothes have to be made from an impermeable material that the employees keep themselves dry. As a safe process, the company has to equip every worker with 2 different working uniforms and 2 shoes. On the other side, the workers have to inform the company if one of these is not anymore valid or usable for the work under the safe working conditions.
